marketDAO

A governance framework where voting rights can be dynamically traded during elections.

The Market-Based Approach to Governance

Unlike traditional DAOs with static voting power, marketDAO introduces market dynamics to collective decision-making, allowing participants to express the strength of their preferences through buying and selling voting power.

With marketDAO, you can:

  • Buy additional voting power for issues you care deeply about
  • Sell your voting tokens if others value them more than you do
  • Speculate on election outcomes through voting token markets
Try the App

Key Features

  • ERC1155-based governance tokens
  • Transferable voting rights during elections
  • Multiple proposal types (resolutions, treasury transfers, token minting)
  • Configurable governance parameters
  • Open-source implementation (MIT License)

Current Status

marketDAO is currently deployed on the Polygon Amoy testnet with a functional web interface.
